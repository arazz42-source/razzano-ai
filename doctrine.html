The Formal Doctrine

RazzanoAI

Version 1.0

Article I

The Foundational Belief

The tax professional is essential.

Not because of compliance.

Because of trust.

Every family’s financial life intersects with a tax advisor. That proximity creates responsibility.

Article II

The Two Laws

Law One

What is true.

All work begins with verified fact. No assumption substitutes for evidence.

Law Two

What is best based on that truth.

Strategy is built only after the foundation is confirmed.

Truth precedes optimization.

Always.

Article III

The Human Core Principle

Technology may accelerate process.

It may not replace judgment.

RazzanoAI exists to strengthen the professional — never to remove them from the equation.

The heart remains human.

Article IV

The Stewardship Standard

A practitioner under RazzanoAI doctrine:

Does not rush.

Does not guess.

Does not shortcut documentation.

Does not sacrifice diligence for volume.

Does not treat clients as transactions.

They operate with deliberate responsibility.

Article V

The Integrity Framework

Every output must:

Be factually verified.

Be legally defensible.

Be ethically sound.

Be strategically intentional.

Protect the client’s long-term interest.

Speed is not a metric of excellence.

Precision is.

Article VI

The AI Governance Principle

AI Must

Reinforce due diligence.

Surface risk.

Clarify decision trees.

Strengthen documentation.

Enhance advisory thinking.

AI Must Never

Fabricate authority.

Encourage shortcutting.

Replace professional oversight.

Remove accountability.

Article VII

The Elevation Mandate

RazzanoAI exists to elevate the profession nationally.

Office by office.
Practitioner by practitioner.

We are not scaling automation.

We are scaling stewardship.

Article VIII

The Closing Standard

The tax professional is entrusted with the financial story of families.

That trust is sacred.

RazzanoAI exists to protect it.

RazzanoAI Doctrine · v1.0

Truth first.

Strategy second.

Integrity always.
